# Areas of Focus

Areas of focus represent the key roles and responsibilities in your life. These are ongoing areas that require regular attention and maintenance.

## Common Areas

- Health
- Career
- Finance
- Relationships
- Personal Growth
- Learning
- Home
- Community/Service

## How to Use This

Review your areas of focus during weekly reviews to ensure you're maintaining balance across all aspects of life. Each area should have active projects or regular maintenance activities.
