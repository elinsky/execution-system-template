# Purpose (Horizon 5)

*The "Why" behind your actions and focus.*

## Essence-Level Purpose

*The ongoing reason for being - manifested continually*

Your core purpose that drives your daily choices and long-term direction. This should answer "Why do I do what I do?"

Examples:
- To create meaningful connections with others
- To serve something greater than myself
- To grow and help others grow
- To bring beauty and order to the world

## Outcome-Level Purpose

*Finite purposes that can be fulfilled and completed*

Specific purposes tied to particular life stages or roles:

- Family-related purposes
- Career-related purposes
- Community-related purposes

## How to Use This

Your purpose provides the "why" that informs your vision (40k), goals (30k), and projects (10k). When making decisions or feeling unclear about direction, return to your purpose for clarity.
