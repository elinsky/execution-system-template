# Home Organization

## 1. PURPOSE - Why This Matters

**Purpose:** Create a more organized and functional living space.

**Why This Matters:** A well-organized home reduces stress and makes daily routines more efficient.

## 2. PRINCIPLES - Standards & Values

**Principles:**

- Keep only items that serve a purpose or bring joy
- Everything should have a designated place
- Maintain systems through regular maintenance

## 3. VISION/OUTCOME - What Success Looks Like

A home where everything has its place and can be found easily. Reduced clutter in main living areas.

## Supporting Resources

* Storage containers for garage
* Label maker for organization

## Ideas to Consider

- Start with one room at a time
- Donate unused items to charity
